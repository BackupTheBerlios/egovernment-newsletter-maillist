LT: Electronic signatures on the move in Lithuania

Date: 2 November 2007
Country: Lithuania
Domain: eGovernment
Topic: Interoperability and infrastructure, eIdentity and eSecurity,
eServices for Citizens

A special government-backed multi-stakeholder programme has developed a
special eSignature which Lithuanians can use for electronic signatures
using their mobile phones.

Information: http://www.epractice.eu/document/4029

SI: Birthday treat for new Slovenian parents

Date: 2 November 2007
Country: Slovenia
Domain: eGovernment
Topic: eIdentity and eSecurity, eServices for Citizens

Three Slovenian hospitals have introduced eNotifications of births, as
part of a new pilot system which will be rolled out to more maternity
wards in the future.

Information: http://www.epractice.eu/document/4031

--------------------

EU: eInvoicing Expert Group set up

Date: 2 November 2007
Country: EU Institutions
Domain: eGovernment
Topic: Interoperability and infrastructure, eProcurement

The European Commission adopted on 31 October a decision setting up an
Expert Group on Electronic Invoicing (eInvoicing). A call for
applications for those wishing to become members of the group is annexed
to the decision, together with the terms of reference. The deadline for
applications is 30 November.

Information: http://www.epractice.eu/document/4027

--------------------

EE: New one-stop portal for Estonia

Date: 2 November 2007
Country: Estonia
Domain: eGovernment
Topic: User centric services, Interoperability and infrastructure,
eServices for Business, eServices for Citizens

The Estonian State portal is planning to merge the former State
Information portal and Citizen portal to create a single integrated
service. Access to information on the new portal will depend on whether
the user is a citizen, entrepreneur or state official.

Information: http://www.epractice.eu/document/4028
